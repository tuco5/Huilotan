Description

Huilotán is an ecological park, located within the municipality of Zapopan and an excellent option for nature lovers.

To get here you have to take the road to Tesistan and turn towards Colotlán for about 10 minutes you will find a viewpoint with a beautiful view of the ravine, from there about 50 meters there is a deviation to the right where you will find a descent to the ecological park.

To access you have to pay $50.00 mxn pesos per person or $80.00 mxn pesos if you want to camp. We have a parking area and many camping areas. It is forbidden disturbing the fauna.
