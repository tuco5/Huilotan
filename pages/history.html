History

Located in a strategic point of the main corridor of the best climbing areas in western Mexico, which includes blocks, sports and traditional routes. Starting from El Escalón, La Loma, Los Kilometers, Peñon de San Lorenzo, Cuajo, Cañon de Huaxtla, Los Colmes and Ixcatán. Within this park there are various blocks in the course of a river and natural jars among vegetation and began as a resting place for climbers in the area.

There are nascent mineral water pools on site, in different areas of the complex.

Mango, lemon, lime, fish production area and purchase of the same on the spot. Small shop selling drinks and basics.
